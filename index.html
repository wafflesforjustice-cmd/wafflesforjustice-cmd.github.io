Waffles for Justice

We're a group of students selling fresh homemade waffles to raise money for the Immigrant Legal Resource Center (ILRC) — an organization that fights for the legal rights of immigrants across the U.S.

For just $2 a waffle, you're helping fund Red Cards, legal training, and real change. After covering our ingredient costs, all profits go directly to the ILRC.

* A small portion of proceeds covers ingredient & supply costs. All remaining profit is donated in full.

The Problem

Inhumane policies.
Real consequences.

"Did you know that 1 in 3 immigrants in the U.S. faces deportation without ever speaking to a lawyer?"

Immigration policies across the U.S. continue to separate families, violate basic human rights, and leave millions without legal support. Our goal is simple: fight these inhumane policies — and ensure every immigrant has access to the legal help they deserve.
